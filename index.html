Calvin Shin

Calvin Shin currently works as a Global Process and Controls Analyst and Interim Project Lead at OpenTable. He currently improves processes by developing macros and developing projects with a Finance Engineeering team.

Calvin is currently attending UC Berkeley's Full Stack Bootcamp program with the improving his work skillset. He's excited to learn more about coding and more languages!

Calvin is also passionate about giving back to the community. He currently works at the nonprofit Gay Asian Pacific Alliance Foundation as a Salesforce implementer. Throughout his time at the foundation, he has chaired the Philanthropy chair and improving the process of providing scholarships and grants to the community. His achievements include increasing the number of applicants by 200% over two years.
